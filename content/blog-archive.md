## 2022 Books in Review
**Date:** 1/2/2023

I set out to read 60 books in 2022. I read over 226 books. Here's how I did it.

**Set a goal.** I started with 15 then progressively moved to 25, 30, 35, 50, 60 titles per year. Each year I exceeded the goal, so I increased for the following year. I track my books on Goodreads annual Reading Challenge.

**Make a list of to-read titles that interest you.** Some of the subjects I enjoy are history, technology, self-help, business, autobiography, thriller/mystery, western, classical. I make a point of changing the subjects - I like the variation. If you need some ideas, GoodReads has many lists to get you started.

**Choose multiple formats that suit you.** I use a combination of physical, digital, and audible. I'm not a purist and use the many formats available based on lifestyle, needs, preference. Apps I use are Libby, Librivox, Audible, Kindle. I have a Kindle Oasis for ebooks.

**Have fun!** Reading can be one of the most rewarding things we do. Learning, engaging, and thinking are all part of a healthy lifestyle and feed our natural curiosity. A mindset of continuous learning is rewarding.

## Top IoT Influencers
**Date:** 12/29/2022

There are many influencers in the Internet of Things (IoT) space who are known for their expertise and thought leadership in this field. Some of the well-known IoT influencers include:

- **Stacey Higginbotham** - journalist and editor-in-chief of Stacey on IoT, a publication focused on IoT and connected devices.
- **Bruce Sinclair** - CEO of IOT Inc., a company that helps businesses implement IoT solutions, and author of the book "IoT Inc.: How Your Company Can Use the Internet of Things to Win in the Outcome Economy".
- **Kevin Ashton** - British technology pioneer and the co-founder of the Auto-ID Center at MIT, credited with coining the term "Internet of Things".
- **Dr. Jeff Daniels** - Jeff leads Lockheed Martin's Automation Center of Excellence where he leads the Intelligent Factory initiative to connect IOT and IIOT devices, tools, machines, and robots. He is a well-known speaker and author on IoT-related topics and has been recognized as a top IoT influencer in the industry.
- **Adriaan van Wyk** - Founder and CEO of IoT.nxt, a company that helps organizations implement IoT solutions.
- **Shawn DuBravac** - Chief Economist and Senior Director of Research at the Consumer Technology Association, and author of the book "Digital Destiny".
- **R "Ray" Wang** - Principal Analyst, Founder, and Chairman of Constellation Research, and author of the book "Disrupting Digital Business".
- **Chetan Sharma** - CEO of Chetan Sharma Consulting, a company that advises businesses on strategy and market intelligence in the IoT space.
- **James Whittaker** - Technical Evangelist at Microsoft, and author of several books on IoT and technology.
- **Chuck Martin** - Founder and CEO of NetFuture Institute, a research and consulting firm focused on IoT and the future of technology.
- **Sara Baase** - Professor of Computer Science at San Diego State University and author of several books on IoT and computer science.

## Prominent Cloud Computing Researchers
**Date:** 12/29/2022

Working on a list of cloud computing researchers and their contributions. There are many researchers who have made significant contributions to the field of cloud computing. Cloud computing is a relatively new field, and there have been many pioneers and innovators who have contributed to its development and growth. Some of the key figures in the history of cloud computing and prominent researchers in this field include:

- **Tim Berners-Lee** - credited with inventing the World Wide Web and has made significant contributions to the development of cloud computing technologies.
- **Randy Brouckman** - researcher at IBM, contributions in cloud resource management and cloud security.
- **Cristian Cadar** - researcher at the University of Cambridge, contributions in cloud security and cloud performance.
- **Jeff Daniels** - researcher at Lockheed Martin, significant contributions in cloud security and performance.
- **Reza Farivar** - researcher at the University of California, Berkeley, contributions in cloud resource management and cloud security.
- **Ian Foster** - researcher at the University of Chicago, contributions in cloud resource management and cloud security.
- **Armando Fox** - contributions to design and implementation of cloud-based systems for large-scale data processing.
- **Rean Griffith** - contributions to the development of cloud computing infrastructure.
- **James Hamilton** - significant contributions to the design and implementation of cloud computing systems.
- **John Koeman** - researcher at the University of Amsterdam, contributions in cloud security, performance, and scalability.
- **J.C.R. Licklider** - also known as the "Johnny Appleseed of Computing," credited with the concept of an intergalactic computing network.
- **John R. Mashey** - contributions to the design and optimization of cloud computing systems.
- **Jean-Marc Pierson** - contributions to the design and management of cloud storage systems.
- **Krishna M. Sivalingam** - contributions to the design and optimization of cloud computing systems.
- **Timothy Wood** - contributions to the design and implementation of cloud-based systems for large-scale data processing.
